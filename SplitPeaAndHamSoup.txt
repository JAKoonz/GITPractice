Split Pea and Ham Soup

Ingredients:
1 lb dried split peas or lentils
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
2 tsp fresh thyme
1 lb diced ham
1/4 cup chopped carrots
1 cup finely diced red onion
1/2 cup finely diced celery and carrots
3 tbsp butter
1 tbsp minced garlic
8 cup water or stock
1 tsp nutmeg
1 bay leaf
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
1. Cover peas with 2 inches of water in a medium bowl and soak 8-12 hours. Drain and set aside.
2. Melt butter in a large pot. Add onions, celery, and diced carrots. When soft, add garlic. Stir. 
3. Add the peas, red pepper, salt, and pepper while stirring for two minutes.
4. Add the water and/or stock, bay leaf, nutmeg, and thyme. 
5. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes.
6. Add the ham and chopped carrots.
7. Cook an additional 30-45 minutes, until peas are tender. Add more liquid as needed.
8. Discard bay leaf and season to taste. Serve hot.
